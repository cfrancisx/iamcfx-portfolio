About Me

I'm Onyemakaogadi Chukwuebuka Francis, known professionally as iamcfx, a creative designer with over 4 years of professional experience in the design industry. My passion lies in creating visually compelling designs that communicate brand stories effectively.

My expertise spans across CorelDRAW, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Canva, allowing me to deliver diverse design solutions for various clients and projects. I specialize in logo design, branding, marketing materials, and digital graphics.

I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics but about solving problems and creating meaningful connections between brands and their audiences. Every project I undertake is approached with creativity, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence.
